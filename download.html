Download The Pyg app!

There are two requirements to be able to download this website as a web app. First you must have an IPhone. Second you must have Safari ( the web browser ) installed. With that said, let's get to it!

Step 1

Tap the chare icon ( located at the bottom of the screen, bang in the middle ).

Step 2

Now scroll down on the menu until you see the "add to home screen" option. Select it.

Step 3

Now tap add on the following prompt. Make sure not to change the name or icon.

Your done!

Now your done and you have succesfully installed the Pyg App. Yaaay!
